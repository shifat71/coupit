What is Coupit?

Coupit offers not only an affiliate program, but will be combining this reward ecosystem with the creation of a giant e-commerce marketplace.

While existing solutions offer to solve just one problem at a time, our team is up to build a secure, useful, & easy-to-use product based on the NEO blockchain. It will include easy cryptocurrency payments integration, and a digital arbitration system.

Coupit will provide an analytics dashboard for sellers to track visitor interaction within their seller pages to help improve engagement efforts.

The Coupit Apps apps

Along with a standalone wallet app, we're also building a global marketplace app. Once you’ve entered into our ecosystem, you can manage everything. Anyone with a smartphone and an internet connection can participate in our global marketplace.

Here at Coupit, we're convinced that blockchain technology with smart contracts will be the core standard for many industries. Smart contracts will be written in NEO to drive exclusive deals with our NEP-5 token.

Our token will leverage blockchain technology to ensure seamless redemption of coupons and allows for crowdsourcing to provide businesses with a platform to increase product exposure.

Frequently asked questions FAQS

Below we’ve provided a list of questions that we're often asked. If you have any other questions, please get in touch using the contact form below.

What happens to unsold tokens?

All remaining tokens will be burned.

When will the tokens be distributed?

Token distribution will begin July 18th.

Where is the transaction id?

Transaction ID can also be recognized as hash. You can find it after you send your NEO. If you do not see it after you send your NEO, you should be able to put your sending address into https://neotracker.io/ to find your transaction id/hash.

How will Recoupit function?

Recoupit will function similar to GAS. We plan to have the release of Recoupit implemented into the Roadmap once the technical details are finalized.

What is the lock up period for the team?

4 months

What is the minimum amount to participate in the ICO? Is there a maximum amount?

1 NEO (or USD equivalent at the time of sale) is the minimum amount required to participate. There is no maximum.

What cryptocurrencies can I use to purchase?

We will accept Bitcoin and NEO cryptocurrencies, Ethereum, Ontology, and EOS, as well as USD through eCheck.

How can I participate in the ICO Token sale?

To participate, we will be releasing a link to register for our whitelist process soon so that participants can be pre-approved.

How do I benefit from the ICO Token?

Coupit is a revolutionary e-commerce platform that relies heavily on blockchain technology to function as a decentralized marketplace. There are many benefits exclusive to the Coupit token, including purchasing ad space and buying products that merchants offer. Additionally, Coupit is being developed as a token pair, along with Recoupit, which generates automatically within wallets holding Coupit tokens. Recoupit can be used to promote products within the coupit app, or take part in special offers provided by Coupit.
